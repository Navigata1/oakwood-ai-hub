Oakwood AI Initiative

12-Week AI Literacy Program

Three tracks. All ages. Zero cost to the church. Week-by-week curriculum built around your community's real needs.

"What are your challenges? What interests you about AI? What scares you about it? Let's start there."
Teaching Philosophy — Meet People Where They Are
Track 1 · Wks 1–4

AI Foundations

"What is this thing and why should I care?"

WK 1

What AI Actually Is

Not magic, not evil, not new. A tool. We strip away the hype and build an honest foundation — what AI can do, what it can't, and why it matters to you personally.

Live: Everyone asks AI a personal question
WK 2

AI in Daily Life

Health, finances, meal planning, family. AI isn't just for tech workers. We explore practical, everyday applications that save time and reduce stress.

Live: Each person solves one real task with AI
WK 3

AI for Communication

Emails, resumes, proposals, grant writing. Learn to express yourself more clearly and professionally — with AI as your writing partner.

Live: Write a grant proposal in 5 minutes
WK 4

AI Safety & Ethics

What to share, what not to, and how to verify. We build healthy skepticism and critical thinking — not fear, but discernment.

Live: Everyone tries to "break" AI
Track 2 · Wks 5–8

Creators & Entrepreneurs

"Now let's build something."

WK 5

AI for Business Ideas

Market research, validation, business plans. Whether you have one idea or ten, AI can help you test them fast and build with confidence.

Live: Validate an idea in 10 minutes
WK 6

Building Without Code

Websites, apps, landing pages — no computer science degree required. The barrier to entry has never been lower. We prove it live.

Live: Build a site from scratch using Replit or Lovable
WK 7

AI for Marketing

Social media, branding, content strategy. You don't need a marketing agency. You need a prompt and a plan.

Live: Create a real campaign for their church or business
WK 8

Voice Agents & Automation

Phone agents, workflows, CRM. AI that works while you sleep — handling calls, scheduling, and follow-ups automatically.

Group project: Build an agent for a local business
Track 3 · Wks 9–12

Ministry & Community

"How does this serve something bigger?"

WK 9

AI for Sermon & Study Prep

Research, outlines, cross-referencing Scripture. AI can help pastors and Sabbath school leaders prepare deeper, richer content in less time.

Live: Prep a Bible study topic with AI assistance
WK 10

AI for Outreach

Event planning, volunteer coordination, communication campaigns. AI amplifies the reach and impact of every ministry effort.

Live: Plan a real upcoming church event together
WK 11

AI for Education

Tutoring, study plans, career development. From K–12 students to adults returning to school, AI becomes a personal academic coach.

Live: Students and parents build personalized plans
WK 12

Showcase & Next Steps

The community presents what they've built over 12 weeks. Celebrate wins, identify gaps, and vote on what to learn next. The cycle continues.

Community showcase + vote on the next cycle
